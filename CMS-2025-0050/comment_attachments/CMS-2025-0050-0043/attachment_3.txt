NCPDP: Ensuring the Heartbeat of
Healthcare is strong
Kelee Petzelt
Advocate for Change | Driving AI to Empower Pharmacists as
Healthcare Leaders | Transforming Patient Care with a Focus…
September 16, 2024
NCPDP (National Council for Prescription Drug Programs, Inc.) , under
the visionary leadership of LeeAnn Stember and her team, has always
understood that healthcare is not just about moving data—it’s
about moving people. It’s about connecting pharmacists with the
information they need to make critical decisions, ensuring patients receive
the right care at the right time, and empowering the entire healthcare
ecosystem to function like a well-coordinated body. In this vision,
pharmacies are the heart of healthcare, and NCPDP is the veins—
pumping the vital flow of data that sustains and strengthens patient care.
Healthcare is personal. Behind every data point and prescription is a
human life depending on the system we create. NCPDP’s role has been to
ensure that the data flow—the “blood” of healthcare—reaches every
corner, empowering pharmacists to not only dispense medications but to
improve outcomes, prevent errors, and enhance overall care.
In today’s rapidly evolving healthcare landscape, value-based care is the
future. The outdated fee-for-service model has disconnected the heart of
healthcare from the rest of the system, leading to inefficiencies and lost
opportunities for better patient outcomes. NCPDP, however, offers the
foundation that reconnects these systems, ensuring that data flows
seamlessly between prescribers, pharmacies, and payers.
This is the human network working together—a network of experts who
are, at their core, people. They care about the patients they serve and
understand that healthcare needs to be human. Without this humanity,

we risk losing the heart of what makes healthcare tick. It is by building on
this personal, human-centered foundation that we ensure the heart of
healthcare remains healthy.
If we don’t make healthcare human, we’ll remain stuck in the inefficiencies
and barriers we face today. Healthcare needs to be value-based, centered
on the well-being of people, and powered by data that enhances patient
care. NCPDP has created the infrastructure—the circulatory system—that
allows this to happen. Through its standards and systems, NCPDP enables
the pharmacist - the heartbeat of healthcare - to remain steady and
strong.
I always thought I didn’t belong at NCPDP before I understood it. I am
no technical genius by any stretch, and that often gets overlooked when
we hear NCPDP—but NCPDP is so much more than technical. It brings
together all parts of the body, working in harmony, and has mastered this
in a way that other standards organizations could stop and learn from. It
may not be the biggest, but the focus, intent, and value of what comes out
of NCPDP is truly worth it. It’s not just about technical standards; it’s about
ensuring the entire healthcare system works in harmony.
Pharmacies, as the heart of care delivery, thrive when they’re integrated
into a broader ecosystem where data flows efficiently, accurately, and with
purpose. By prioritizing human connection and enabling real-time data
exchange, NCPDP ensures that pharmacists have the tools to meet quality
measures, participate in value-based care, and deliver more personalized
patient care.
Here’s the key difference: NCPDP, as the circulatory system, enhances and
complements the other standards organizations. While groups like HL7,
X12, and others provide the framework for clinical and administrative data
exchange, NCPDP ensures that pharmacy and medication-related data
flows seamlessly through the system. It doesn’t compete with these
organizations; it strengthens them. By making sure that pharmacy is not
overlooked, NCPDP is making healthcare more complete, more connected,
and more capable of providing the best outcomes for patients.
This powerful analogy of NCPDP as the veins circulating lifeblood to the
heart of healthcare should resonate with all members—pharmacists,
healthcare leaders, and those yet to be involved. It reminds us that the
future of healthcare is not just about advancing technology; it’s about
ensuring that every human element of the system works together to
create a healthier, more compassionate world.
NCPDP has laid the foundation for this future. If the heart of healthcare is
strong, every other part of the system can function at its best. The blood
flow of data that NCPDP enables will ensure that pharmacists and the
broader healthcare system can grow, thrive, and deliver the kind of care
patients deserve—care that’s both human and efficient, data-driven yet
deeply personal. Together, we can ensure that the heartbeat of healthcare
is healthy, allowing the entire system to function, thrive, and provide the
best possible outcomes for everyone.
