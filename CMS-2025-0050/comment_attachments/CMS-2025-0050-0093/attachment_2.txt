We need a change Solution and Features iPill ecosystem features: •A secure dispenser preﬁlled with opioids or OUD meds •delivered directly to Vets’ homes •secure biometric controlled home storage of meds •precise dispensing at home so only the prescription holder can access meds at only the prescribed schedule •remote medication adherence monitoring to detect missed daily doses in real-time •Alerts to family, friends, caregivers to remediate missed meds •destruction of unused pills at 30 days or immediately if tampered to prevent abuse,, diversion or accidental overdose •remote vital sign monitoring via smart phone faceprint •telehealth for remote psychosocial support Competitive Advantage iPill is the ﬁrst integrated pill dispenser delivered to the home to monitor both patients and pills. IPill provides holistic treatment with psychosocial support of the “mind” via telehealth and “physical” support with meds via precise home dispensing. Because iPill destroys unused pills at 30 days or immediately if tampered, it can be used to prevent misuse, diversion, and accidental overdoses. Further, because any shape or size of pills can be used in the iPill, it has multiple customers and multiple revenue streams including jails, OUD clinics, VA, Contract Research organizations (CROs) and specialty pharmacy for HIV drugs, cancer drugs, or transplant drugs and sedatives, tranquilizer, and stimulants. Most signiﬁcantly, iPill is avoiding reliance on commercial insurance for revenue.
