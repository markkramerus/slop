To Whom It May Concern:
I appreciate the opportunity to submit comments in response to the Request for
Information (RFI) issued by the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) in
partnership with the Assistant Secretary for Technology Policy/Office of the National
Coordinator for Health Information Technology (ASTP/ONC). As Director of Information
Technology for the South Carolina Primary Health Care Association and Program Director
for the South Carolina Health Center Controlled Network (SCHCCN), I am committed to
advancing equitable, secure, and patient-centered digital health innovation for Community
Health Centers and the underserved populations they serve.

Establish a Centralized Digital Health Resource Hub: CMS should support the creation of a
