Our platform offers all the tools necessary for a seamless healthcare visit, designed to deliver a superior online experience compared to traditional physical visits.The Patient’s App Easy Sign-Up and Payment: Patients can sign up and manage payments through the app, including Medicaid account authentication. Instant Virtual Visits: The app enables instant face-to-face visits with virtual doctors, followed by human Care Providers if needed. Appointment Scheduling: Patients can schedule future appointments directly through the app. User Ratings: The system allows patients to rate their experience with practitioners using a 5-star system similar to Uber. Prescription Management: The app handles prescription refill requests, tracks current medications, and provides access to lab results. 
Practitioners' Website Dashboard Access: Practitioners can log in to view their dashboard for managing appointments, patient records, chat, and notifications. Flexible Scheduling: Like Uber drivers, practitioners can set their work hours and create their schedules to suit their lifestyle. Virtual Care Options: Practitioners can offer both instant & scheduled virtual care. Patient Management: They have access to patient history and can write prescriptions and request lab work. Integrated Tools: The site is integrated with EPIC for patient data management, and API plugins automate transcription with ICD-10 diagnosis codes, helping care providers save time and increase revenue. 
Administration Website Multi-Level Access: The administration platform supports login with multiple access levels.
