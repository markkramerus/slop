Who We Are &
What We Do?
MEDA is a patented AI-enabled disruptive digital health
company that is transforming healthcare by providing artificial
human doctors on an iPhone or Android device to discuss your
healthcare needs. We leverage AI trained on healthcare
diagnosis data. Once a diagnosis hypothesis is created, the
patient is immediately connected to a human doctor along with
the ICD-10 diagnosis codes, so the doctor understands the
patient's needs and can quickly provide care. This cuts over 50%
of the time a doctor needs to spend with a patient. In addition
to offering a “Doctor in your Pocket” 24/7, Our patented
technology also offers a complete solution for the preoperative,
intraoperative, and postoperative management of patients. This
allows healthcare providers to streamline workflows, enhance
clinical efficiency, and focus more on patient outcomes.
