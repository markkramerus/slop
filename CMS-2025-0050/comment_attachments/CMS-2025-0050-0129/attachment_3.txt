MEDA: Revolutionizing Digital Healthcare
Overview
MEDA is a digital health company with the mission to "digitize, democratize, and disrupt healthcare,"
aiming to provide instant, affordable care to over a billion people in the next 10 years. Founded by Scott
Corlett in 2022, MEDA leverages AI and machine learning to transform how healthcare is delivered,
offering patients access to virtual healthcare via AI-driven avatars.

Technology
MEDA’s platform centers around a patented virtual assistant that uses machine learning to adapt
responses and facial expressions, providing a human-like interaction. The AI technology offers a complete
solution for preoperative, intraoperative, and postoperative patient management, streamlining intake,
enhancing education, and supporting recovery. The avatars facilitate immediate healthcare interactions,
matching patients with appropriate practitioners and improving accessibility regardless of geography or
economic status.

Business Model & Market Position
MEDA is poised to capitalize on the growing demand for telemedicine and AI-driven healthcare,
particularly in underserved markets. By integrating digital avatars that can handle various medical queries
and direct patients to the appropriate care, MEDA eliminates many of the inefficiencies of traditional
healthcare. The company is well-positioned to disrupt the market, offering an affordable and scalable
solution that can reach a global audience.

Vision & Future Goals
MEDA’s goal is to become a leader in AI-powered healthcare by offering innovative, technology-driven
solutions that transform how medical care is accessed. By reducing barriers to care, the platform aims to
create a more equitable healthcare system globally.
