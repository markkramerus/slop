WHY
To digitize, democratize, and disrupt healthcare,
making affordable and instant care accessible to over
a billion people, regardless of geography or income,
within the next decade.
HOW
• By leveraging patented AI technology to
create human-like virtual doctors that
reduce costs and enhance patient
experiences.
• By integrating machine learning and
video synthesis with a seamless
telemedicine platform, ensuring efficient,
secure, and scalable healthcare delivery.
• By partnering with Medicaid and
healthcare providers to build a system
optimized for accessibility and affordability.
WHAT
MEDA offers an AI-driven healthcare platform with
customizable virtual assistants that provide immediate
assessments, automate diagnoses, and connect patients to
practitioners for timely, efficient care. This platform improves
outcomes, lowers costs, and scales healthcare delivery
globally.
