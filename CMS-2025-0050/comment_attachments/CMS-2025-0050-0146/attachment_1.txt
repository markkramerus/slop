Subject: Public Comment on CMS-2025-0050-0069 – Health Technology Ecosystem
Dear Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services,
I appreciate the opportunity to provide feedback on the Health Technology Ecosystem Request
for Information (CMS-2025-0050-0069). The modernization of digital health infrastructure and
data interoperability is essential for improving healthcare access, efficiency, and patient
outcomes.
CMS’ commitment to enhancing digital health capabilities will empower Medicare
beneficiaries with better tools to manage their health while supporting providers with streamlined
data exchange. The proposed initiative includes the development of a national provider
directory, modernized identity verification, expanded Blue Button 2.0 API, and trusted
data exchange—are critical steps toward a more connected and efficient healthcare system.
I encourage CMS to continue fostering innovation in digital health while ensuring accessibility,
security, and interoperability remain top priorities. These advancements will not only improve
patient care but also reduce administrative burdens and enhance healthcare delivery nationwide.
Thank you for your leadership in shaping the future of healthcare technology. I appreciate the
opportunity to contribute to this important discussion.
Sincerely,
Megan Carney-Tardio, MSN, RN for 25 years and DNP candidate.
