Executive Summary
Salient Health appreciates the opportunity to respond to CMS and ASTP/ONC's Request for
Information on the Health Technology Ecosystem. As a purpose-built healthcare decision
intelligence platform, Salient is uniquely positioned to advance CMS' vision for a modern,
patient-centric, interoperable digital health ecosystem. Our technology delivers real-time,
transaction-level insights to government agencies, payers, providers, and value-based care
organizations, enabling tangible improvements in cost efficiency, fraud reduction, access to data,
and quality of care.
Over the past decade, our solution has supported the largest Medicaid integrity initiative in the
U.S., helping New York State recover over $1.7 billion in improper payments, improve audit
accuracy, and support delivery reform initiatives. Salient delivers a vertically integrated, end-to-
end analytics solution that enables non-technical users to interrogate data, discover root causes,
and take informed action within seconds—without relying on IT.
We believe Salient should be identified and recognized as a strategic partner for CMS to:
• Advance interoperability through actionable, real-time analytics
• Support API-based data sharing and patient empowerment
• Enable providers and care teams to engage with full-patient context
• Drive ROI in VBC models and population health outcomes
• Operationalize data to reduce friction, complexity, and costs
Section TD-1 / VB-3 / PR-7: Technology Contributions to the Ecosystem
Salient Health offers a full-stack analytics platform tailored for healthcare, with native ingestion,
integration, modeling, contextualization, and visualization capabilities. Unlike fragmented BI
stacks (e.g., Power BI + Snowflake + ETL tools), our architecture delivers immediate insights
and actionable intelligence from source-level data.
Key technical contributions:

• A proprietary in-memory database (UXT) optimized for high-speed healthcare queries at
scale
• Batch ingestion and real-time modeling of claims, EMR, encounter, and social
determinant data
• Zero-code, drill-down exploration from population trend to patient, encounter, and claim
level
• Embedded collaboration, root cause analysis, and operationalization features
Our solution powers state and county Medicaid programs, OMIG, ACOs, and health systems.
Users report 6x improvements in audit productivity, 70% user adoption rates, and multi-million-
dollar recoveries from fraud and abuse.
Section PC-8 / PA-2 / TD-2: Enhancing Data Access & Interoperability
Salient simplifies access to complex, siloed datasets and makes them usable by frontline staff,
program managers, and clinicians. Our platform fully supports:
• FHIR-based services
• Standard REST APIs
• Bulk data transfers
• Integration with QHINs, HIEs, and EMRs
We recommend CMS expand Blue Button 2.0 to include:
• Encounter-level context
• Price transparency data
• Risk scores and quality metrics
• Social determinants of health (SDOH)
CMS should further incentivize the use of integrated intelligence tools that reduce technical debt
and eliminate the need for external ETL pipelines.
Section VB-1 / VB-2 / VB-4: Empowering Value-Based Care Organizations
Salient has supported DSRIP, PCMH, Health Homes, and ACO-like entities across New York
State. Our Health Home and Population Health Management modules consolidate:
• Clinical, claims, and utilization data
• Attribution models
• Risk stratification
• Performance benchmarking

This enables VBC entities to:
• Identify high-risk cohorts
• Monitor cost and outcome trends in real time
• Support care coordination
• Detect anomalies and opportunities for intervention
Our platform is used by PPS leaders and Medicaid directors for executive oversight and
performance tracking of 70+ initiatives. Salient's visual intelligence environment eliminates
latency in decision-making, empowering VBC participants to act.
Section PR-2 / PR-7 / TD-9: Reducing Burden & Enabling Action
Salient enables non-technical staff to perform data exploration and root cause analysis without
needing SQL or dev support. Our visual platform supports clinical quality reporting, claims
audits, and care navigation via:
• Direct access to integrated, contextualized data
• Embedded logic and reusable filters
• Bookmarkable insights and sharing tools
This significantly reduces provider burden, audit cycles, and time-to-insight. By integrating
metrics and workflows, Salient reduces redundancy and enables value-focused reporting.
Section TD-5 / TD-6 / TD-12: Recommendations to CMS
We recommend CMS:
• Expand incentives for vertically integrated platforms like Salient that enable real-time
operational intelligence
• Promote interoperability standards that align with embedded analytics and operational
tools
• Establish performance-based evaluation criteria (e.g., ROI, audit lift, user adoption) for
vendor selection
• Support tools that are proven in Medicaid settings and deliver measurable fraud, waste,
and abuse prevention

Salient is already delivering on the goals articulated in this RFI. Our track record with the New
York Medicaid program and others demonstrates:
• Scalable, secure data infrastructure
• High-speed, multi-source integration
• Operationalization of insights for program managers and providers
• Quantifiable financial impact and improved outcomes
We respectfully submit Salient Health as a proven partner and innovative leader for CMS to
