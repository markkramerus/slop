Dear Administrator Brooks-LaSure,
Sifter Solutions, Inc. appreciates the opportunity to submit feedback in response to CMS's Request for
Information regarding national standards for the exchange of electronic health information, specifically
as they relate to digital health products and their role in supporting improved patient outcomes,
interoperability, and health equity.
As a digital health company with deep roots in online grocery and food-as-medicine innovation, our
mission is simple: Making Healthy Grocery Shopping Easy. Sifter provides a platform that connects
individual health needs to personalized grocery guidance using real-time diet-matching technology,
enabling everything from barcode scanning to condition-specific food prescriptions and benefit
redemption.
We believe that digital health tools addressing diet-related chronic conditions must be seamlessly
integrated into provider workflows, administrative systems, and benefit delivery channels. Our
responses to questions PR-1 through PR-4 reflect this vision and provide practical, tech-forward
recommendations based on direct experience implementing medically tailored grocery programs across
health plans and providers nationwide.
We would appreciate the opportunity to collaborate with CMS on this critical conversation regarding
future strategies under the 21st Century Cures Act.
