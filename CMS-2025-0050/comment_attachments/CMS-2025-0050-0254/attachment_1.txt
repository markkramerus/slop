The U.S. still lacks a comprehensive, nationwide FHIR terminology server that: •Supports $expand, $lookup, $validate-code across all terminologies, •Enables versioning, subsumption testing, and multilingual support, •And allows live integration into EHRs, payers, and public health systems. CMS should support adoption of these APIs as part of future USCDI+ genomics efforts, and align them with broader TEFCA implementation.
