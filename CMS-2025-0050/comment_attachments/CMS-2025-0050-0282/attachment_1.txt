Regarding CMS TEAM (Transforming Episode Accountability Model) it is important
that the model remain mandated rather than voluntary to encourage participation
that is key towards the priorities of CMS:
● Accountability and driving choice
● Reduction in healthcare spend
● Use of next-generation AI for patient outcomes and quality of care
Rainfall Health’s platform is purpose-built to reduce the burden on providers and
health systems while accelerating data availability and exchange. In particular, we
address:
● PR-7: How shared workflows and aligned metrics can drive provider and
payer collaboration around interoperability; and
● PR-8: How CMS can simplify clinical quality data responsibilities through
automation and smart integration.
We strongly encourage CMS to prioritize partnerships with vendors who design their
technologies to mirror CMS mandates and integrate directly into existing EMR
systems. Interoperability must not come at the cost of additional complexity.
Platforms like Rainfall Health’s—which embed compliance, data transparency, and

reimbursement optimization into existing provider workflows—help health systems
operationalize CMS models without disruption.
We offer three key recommendations:
1. Deliver Real-Time, Mandate-Aligned Data: CMS should ensure timely data
delivery aligned with its quality and reimbursement models, in a standardized
format. This enables proactive compliance and outcome-driven care and
additional synthesizing and insights that can be gleaned through AI.
2. Promote Shared, Measurable Workflows: Standardized, AI-enabled
workflows and metrics can motivate providers and payers to collaborate on
performance improvement and cost reduction. This framework standard
brings together many provider groups alongside hospitals to create one
framework for the patient.
3. Reward Interoperable, Non-Disruptive Solutions: Vendors that integrate
with existing infrastructures—rather than require costly platform
overhauls—should be central to CMS’s digital health strategy. Consolidating
data for reporting and insights instead of implementing costly changes.
Rainfall Health was created to help hospitals and providers meet the demands of
CMS programs with clarity, accountability, and efficiency. By enabling continuous
data monitoring and operational alignment, our platform supports the very
outcomes CMS aims to scale: better care, smarter spending, and healthier
populations.
We appreciate the opportunity to contribute to this critical conversation and
welcome the chance to further engage with CMS in advancing a more connected,
intelligent, and accountable health technology ecosystem.
