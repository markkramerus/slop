June 13, 2025 Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services Re: Docket (CMS -2025 -0050) Digital Health RFI On behalf of the Massachusetts Association of Behavioral Health Systems (MABHS), a statewide organization of 53 Psychiatric and Substance Use facilities , we are offering these comments for your consideration relative to Docket (CMS -2025 -0050) Digital Health RFI . These comments are focused on our request for CMS to reinstate remote virtual services for Partial Hospital Programs (PHP) Many of our hospitals offer Partial Hospital services , and during the Public Health Emergency , our patients benefitted enormously through Medicare’s coverage of virtual PHP services. Unfortunately, Medicare members are not currently afforded the same virtual treatment options as non -Medicare members, unless they are able to pay out of pocket. We urge CMS to reinstate virtual PHP services for Medicare beneficiaries for the following reasons: • Virtual PHP achieves similar clinically equivalent outcomes such as reduction in symptoms, improved functioning, and participant satisfaction as in -person care; • Virtual PHP improves attendance and reduces barriers such as transportation, caregiving responsibilities, disability barriers such as for wheelchair bound patients, and access despite geographic distance or rural isolation ; • Virtual PHP is beneficial for those who suffer from disabilities and chronic medical conditions that make in -person attendance extremely difficult. Additionally, those who may have a contagious illness can participate in virtual PHP without jeopardizing others ; • Virtual PHP is beneficial to workforce retention for staff who are at risk of leaving the field due to health concerns , caregiving responsibilities, or geographic constraints ; • Finally, Virtual PHP is beneficial to maintaining continuity of care for discharged patients following a psychiatric hospitalization , thereby helping to reduce more costly inpatient re -admissions. We respectfully request that CMS consider reinstating Virtual PHP for the benefit of Medicare members . This population would benefit greatly by having this option where in - person programming is not feasible or preferred . Please let me know if you have any questions or need any further information.
