Creating A Developer Marketplace And
Expanding The ADDI Platform
A Certified Developer Ecosystem for Personalizing Addison Care
Electronic Caregiver is positioned to launch a powerful new extension of its AWS-certified ADDI
Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) by opening a Developer Marketplace that enables third-party
developers to build and monetize features for the Addison Care experience within a secure,
compliant, and curated environment.
This expansion leverages the existing RESTful API infrastructure and core PaaS services to allow
certified partners to integrate directly with Addison’s 3D interactive avatar, scene engine, and
health automation framework - dramatically accelerating innovation, driving revenue growth, and
enhancing the personalization of care delivery.
STRATEGIC BENEFITS
1.Accelerated Innovation Through 3rd-Party Development
• Extend Addison’s capabilities across specialty conditions, behavioral wellness, cultural preferences, engagement
modules, and emerging care protocols without consuming internal R&D cycles.
• Developers build once on the ADDI PaaS, with sandbox environments and API access governed by certification
protocols.
2.New High-Margin Revenue Streams
• Monetize premium features as add-ons, monthly upgrades, or enterprise enablement tools.
• ECG benefits from revenue share, platform fees, and developer tier subscriptions.
3.Enterprise Value Uplift: Platform vs. Product
• Elevates Addison from a product to a customizable ecosystem, increasing valuation multiples.
• Creates long-term stickiness by embedding partners, developers, and enterprise clients deeper into the platform.
4.Increased Personalization & User Control
• Users (or caregivers) can enhance Addison through an in-app menu:
• New games for cognitive health
• Language or faith-based routines
• Expanded vitals and disease-specific care modules
• Virtual seasonal themes, music, voice packs, and scenes
