Executive Summary
Verve, LLC proposes an AI-powered Integrated Digital Health Communication Platform
designed to transform how Medicare Beneficiaries access and understand their healthcare
information. This platform will also enable other Medicare stakeholders such as CMS
employees, researchers, and contractors to access information for their work or develop other
solutions to support Medicare beneficiaries. By leveraging advanced artificial intelligence and
integrating with existing CMS infrastructure, this platform addresses critical communication
gaps while supporting CMS's digital transformation and meeting cost-effective technology
objectives.
Our innovative solution directly responds to CMS's Request for Information on Health
Technology Ecosystem which has the underlying benefit of beneficiary engagement; reducing
provider burden; increasing price transparency; and proactive fraud detection.
A. Patients and Caregivers
PC-1. Patient Needs - Health Management and Care Navigation Apps
a. Top Digital Health Capabilities for Medicare Beneficiaries:
Our proposed platform would enable Medicare beneficiaries to:
• Simplified claims understanding: Receive clear, plain English, personalized
explanations of their Medicare benefits; enrollment assistance; coverage questions
• Proactive notifications: Get timely alerts about important activities and coverage
information
• Intelligent assistance: Access 24/7 support for common Medicare questions and
concerns
• Enhanced transparency: Better understand healthcare costs related to beneficiaries and
coverage decisions
b. Personal Health Assistant Capabilities:
Our research indicates Medicare beneficiaries most frequently need help with:
• Understanding what Medicare covers for specific services (for example, how much out-
of-pocket expense prior to receiving the services).

• Interpreting Medicare Summary Notices and explanations of benefits

Our AI-powered system would address these needs through intuitive, conversational interactions.

• Phase 2: Expansion to provider related services such as, FFS updates, prior authorization,

• Fraud detection and prevention impact
• Reduction in routine customer service inquiries
Technical Framework
Core Capabilities:
1. Advanced AI Engine: Natural language processing for beneficiary, provider and CMS
interactions
2. Data Integration Platform: Seamless connection with existing CMS infrastructure
3. Notification Services: Proactive alerts for important account changes
Security and Privacy:
• Multi-factor authentication with existing Medicare.gov credentials
• End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions
• Comprehensive audit logging and monitoring
• Granular privacy controls for beneficiary data access
Fraud, Waste, and Abuse Detection
Key FWA Protection Areas:
1. Phantom Services: Beneficiaries reporting services they never received
2. Identity Protection: Unusual access patterns or unauthorized information requests
3. Provider Oversight: Patterns suggesting inappropriate billing practices
4. Beneficiary Education: Helping patients recognize and report suspicious activities
AI-Enhanced Capabilities:
• Advanced pattern analysis for unusual claims or access patterns
• Behavioral monitoring for abnormal account activities
• Predictive modeling to identify high-risk scenarios
• Real-time validation of beneficiary-reported concerns
