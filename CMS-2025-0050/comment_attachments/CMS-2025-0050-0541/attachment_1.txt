HealthyConnect Intelligent Network Layer
Notes to Architecture:
a. A Universal health record is elusive and our approach is to build as real time as possible dynamic
care plan and loop patient engagement into new model of care leveraging the existing assets
attributable to a patient.
b. AI is capable of assimilating this type of data rapidly.
c. AI can also link other data sources (not shown) such as educational content.
d. AI communications can be configured multi-modal to easily interact with almost any patient (voice,
text, e-mail, secure chat) in HIPAA compliant formats.
e. Workflows can be created rapidly too and route via existing pathways.
f. Quality metrics (now shown) can also be a data source, a scorecard of compliance requirements.
