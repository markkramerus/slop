We believe every American should have access to a dynamic care plan, powered by AI, continuously updated through real -time data streams including EMR, HIE, pharmacy (Surescripts), and wearable integrations. We propose that CMS adopt the goal of enabling a Dynamic and Interactive Care Plan as a national standard. Static care summaries or one -time instructions are insufficient for modern, complex care. A dynamic care plan, like that already used in HealthyConnect, is responsive, continuously updated, and serves as the foundation for better o utcomes, more meaningful caregiver coordination, and real -time decision support. This care plan should be visible to the patient, accessible to authorized caregivers, and integrated across providers and settings. We operate under a simple but powerful idea: every patient deserves 24/7 care guidance without burdening the healthcare system. Our platform enables providers to serve more patients, reduces readmissions and medication errors, and connects patients and car egivers to real -time, actionable insight. The care system CMS envisions isn’t just possible —it’s operational. We welcome the opportunity to partner with CMS to scale a modern, equitable, digitally native infrastructure. A.
