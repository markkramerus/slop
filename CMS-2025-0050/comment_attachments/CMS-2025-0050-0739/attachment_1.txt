Taking on a wide -scale IT modernization gets at the underlying issue with the healthcare system,and making healthcare work better for everyone requires tackling a variety of areas. Although Personal Health Records (PHRs) were designed to give patients comprehensive access to their medical information, the current ecosystem lacks standardization, interoperability, and security safeguards.
