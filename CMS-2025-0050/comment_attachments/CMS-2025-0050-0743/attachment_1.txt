F r a u d involves intentional deception or misrepresentation to gain an unauthorized benefit. W a s t e refers to the careless, inefficient, or unnecessary use of resources that doesn't involve criminal intent. Waste stems from mismanagement rather than malice. A b u s e involves practices that are inconsistent with sound business or medical practices, resulting in unnecessary costs or reimbursements.
