The current state of hospital care relies on manual, intermittent snapshots of vital sign information once
every 4 - 6 hours by a healthcare workforce that is challenged by staffing shortages, which jeopardizes
patient safety, and drives up the cost of care. In the home setting, family caregivers are unreasonably
burdened with the responsibility and cost of caring for loved ones yet have little means of recognizing the
evolving complications associated with post-acute and/or chronic conditions.
In both settings, patient deterioration is commonly unrecognized until the patient “crashes,” triggering the
rapid response team (RRT) in the hospital, or a call to 911 and an ambulance trip to the emergency room
from home. The inevitable consequences of this antiquated practice include transfers to the ICU,
increased hospital mortality, increased hospital length of stay (LOS), increased healthcare workforce
stress, burn-out, and labor expense, increased emergency room utilization, increased hospital
readmissions, patient and family dissatisfaction, and skyrocketing increases in the total cost of healthcare
to the US taxpayer and society.
A medical grade wearable for automated continuous patient monitoring is available today
that solves each of these problems!

The BioButton Continuous Patient Monitoring System provides timely, algorithmic-based alerts as an
early warning of meaningful changes in a patient’s vital signs to save valuable staff time, allow for
proactive clinical interventions and reduce the burden of manual charting. The BioDashboard clinical
triage and intelligence system enables clinicians to receive near real-time trend analysis to help identify
potential deterioration at an earlier time-frame. This system provides a comprehensive and scalable end-
to-end remote care solution, to support rapid deployments across the healthcare continuum, from in-
hospital to at-home.
• FDA-cleared wearable technology: The BioButton multi-parameter wearable device combines an
effortless ‘stick it on and forget it’ user experience with medical grade clinical accuracy that healthcare
professionals can trust. It passively captures high frequency vital signs, physiologic biometrics and
symptomatic event trends on a discreet single-use, rechargeable device. The BioButton is
configurable with acute and post-acute modes to address patient monitoring needs that span both
care settings and acuity levels.
• Advanced analytics and data services: Continuous medical grade monitoring with the BioButton
device automatically captures high frequency vital sign measurements, powering the BioCloud
advanced analytics engine for earlier detection of subtle changes in a patients’ physiologic trending
status. This passive remote capture of medical grade data allows health systems to scale remote
care making the home an extension of the healthcare system, while automating vital sign collection at
the point-of-care, to improve clinical workflow efficiency, prioritization of care and resource allocation.
